Hello, I'm Gustavo Macedo!

I specialize in artificial intelligence, science diplomacy and innovation. Speaker and business consultant. Professor of administration and economics at INSPER, and professor of IBMEC international relations. UN consultant for artificial intelligence, and researcher at USP in innovation, with vast experience inside and outside Brazil in the public and private sector. Formed first place at UNICAMP/University of London, I have a doctorate and master's degree from USP/Columbia University of Nova York. I was a visiting researcher in the USA, Canada, Belgium and Hungary, in addition to working as a researcher at New York UN Atrocity Prevention Department. I frequently collaborate with national and international conference on current themes.

About my research

Over the past 15 years my research agenda has focused on topics such as international politics, development, human rights and technology. I have always valued the balance between theory and practice. In 2019, After passing through some of the main universities and diplomatic organizations in the world, I returned to Brazil to work and teach about the impacts of artificial intelligence on our lives, companies and communities. Since 2023, I have been part of the UN team to evaluate artificial intelligence in Brazil. My life and my work as a teacher and researcher are dedicated to transforming a more prosperous and fair.
